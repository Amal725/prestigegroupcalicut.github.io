About Prestige Ocean Pearl

On Beach Road in Calicut, Prestige Ocean Pearl is a masterfully designed residential community spread across 6.5 acres. With four 30-story towers, Prestige Ocean Pearl has 530 exquisitely designed luxury houses with a combination of 3 and 4 bedrooms. The towers are further elevated by means of a parking deck that rises four levels above ground to provide maximum seclusion and unhindered views of the sea. Its beautifully landscaped facade ascends to a raised elevation that affords uninterrupted views of the sea. In addition, it shields homes from the noise and traffic of the main road. All of the houses have two or more external faces, which promotes cross ventilation and provides for natural light and ventilation. A multipurpose hall, a gym, a swimming pool, indoor activities, and a clubhouse are just a few of the recreational features provided by Prestige Ocean Pearl. At Prestige Ocean Pearl, sustainability is ingrained in the architecture, with features including rainwater collection systems, solar lighting for communal areas, and electric vehicle charging stations. There is also special parking for people with disabilities.

Our Projects

Prestige White Meadows

Exclusive ultra-luxury villas are available in Whitefield at Prestige White Meadows, a neighbourhood for the very wealthy. Prestige White Meadows, a development of 288 Sky Villas in four high-rise towers with a maximum of 27 floors, and 66 Independent Bungalows set against a picturesque backdrop, is located on a gentle hillock in Whitefield, one of Bangalore's most coveted suburbs. Together, these residences paint a picture of a blissfully opulent neighbourhood that rests in undisputed majesty. The high rise towers are positioned on the flatter land surrounding Prestige White Meadows, while the Independent Bungalows are situated on tiered terraces defining the area's steady ascent. The Independent Bungalows are three-story buildings with floorspaces ranging from 5576 square feet to 6219 square feet that are situated in picturesque urban settings.

Prestige Windsor Park

A house that was created with your needs in mind to the greatest standards. Prestige Windsor Park, a joint venture between Prestige Group, NuTech Associates, and Late MS Sammandappa & Family, will consist of 637 residential condominiums spread across 5.77 acres on the Poonamallee High Road in Vanagaram, Chennai. For starters, the location is ideal because it is close to the top hospitals, restaurants, shopping centres, banks, and more. It is also close to schools, colleges, and other institutions of higher learning. Moreover, arterial roads make it simple to reach the airport and the Metro train station. Another reason is that your home at Prestige Windsor Park is constructed in accordance with Vaastu's rules. You will benefit from an abundance of natural lighting and ventilation, which is made possible by the apartment buildings' thoughtful placement relative to one another, which allows for an unrestricted flow of air and light in all directions. You will delight in unadulterated pleasure of vast planted open areas in Prestige's distinctive style, making it a great location for you to interact and mingle with your neighbours while offering you the benefit of living close to nature. Finally, Prestige Windsor Park's opulent clubhouse offers a wide range of lifestyle amenities at your disposal to keep your free time both productive and enjoyable.
